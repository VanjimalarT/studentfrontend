The Trail

Your study journey, mapped out

SkillSprint follows the same path every time you learn something new — from building a deck to proving you'll still remember it next month. Scroll to walk the trail.

Stage 01 — Build

Create your deck

Group flashcards by subject, unit, or exam. Bring your own notes or start from a blank deck — SkillSprint keeps everything organized in one place.

01
Stage 02 — Sprint

Review flashcards

Quick question-and-answer recall drills, timed and focused. Every card you flip tells SkillSprint a little more about what's actually sticking.

02
Stage 03 — Log

Track your sessions

Every sprint gets logged automatically, so you can see your streak build day by day and turn studying into a habit instead of a scramble.

03
Stage 04 — Measure

Check retention metrics

See exactly which topics are sticking and which ones are fading, with a clear breakdown of recall accuracy over time — not just a study log.

04
Stage 05 — Repeat

Resurface what's fading

Cards you're close to forgetting resurface automatically, right before you'd lose them — closing the loop back to Stage 01 with a smarter deck.

05
Why It Works

Built on spaced repetition

Cramming feels productive and fades fast. SkillSprint spaces your reviews out so recall gets stronger — and more durable — every time.
